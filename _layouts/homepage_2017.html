// About Me

I am both a UX/UI designer and front-end engineer specializing in creative website and user interface solutions. I have a wide array of experience from working with digital agencies to working with emerging SaaS marketing solutions.

My journey into the world of design began in photography, in the dark room of my high school where I learned the beauty of unique composition and the simplicity of seeing the world in black and white. My passion for technology led me into the world of front-end engineering where I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, and javascript. I specialize in creative technology solutions, responsive web design, and UI development for businesses large and small. I am experienced in CMS development as well as front end MVC frameworks.

My expertise and passion has led me to brand myself a full-stack designer, where I can own a project end to end, and blur the line that separates the machine from the visceral.
